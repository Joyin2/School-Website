MISSION AND GOAL


VISION


At Universal Knowledge Park School , Silchar , we try our best to instill the educational and co-educational experience that is high class in every respect and which prepares children for ideal citizenship. We believe that every child is unique . Every child is allowed to grow as an individual and thereby explore his / her full potential. Through our innovative techniques we strive to expose our students to stimulating and enriching learning experience
The School’s vision is to provide a better learning environment to encourage the children to bring out the best in themselves and be confident along with their all round development through discovering the joy and the art of learning and instilling abiding values in themselves.

Our Motto Nurturing Minds, Nurturing Talents

MISSION


Our mission is to foster each child's well-being as a foundation for academic and life success by drawing on the strengths of the child's ...entire community. To provide and teach the use of modern tools, materials and technology and by applying play-way method .
To equally treat all the students coming from different backgrounds and cultures.
Work collaboratively with fellow staffs , students, parents and the community.
To make them proactive thinker and molding a generation competent and creative individuals capable of proving constructive leadership in a new world order.

GOALS


To provide a safe, secure and joyful environment in which each child can dare to dream, enjoy learning, achieve excellence and become a life long learner.
To provide opportunities for children to step beyond the confines of classroom learning and the immediate context to explore new places and adventures.
To inculcate in children , the value of compassion, empathy and respect towards other and the environment
To encourage children to develope the qualities of self discipline , positive self image , team work , spirit of adventure and leadership .
To inspire young minds to think creatively and laterally, solve problems and develop a lifelong enthusiasm for learning,
To make children aware and appreciative of their history, cultures and traditions , whilst being open to other culture and alternative views of the world .
Here’s what sets us apart UKPS was conceived as an educational institution with a belief that every child is unique and special . They are the future of our nation and its our responsibility to nurture their minds and talents not only for the betterment of our nation but also for better humanitarian.
We believe that a teacher should play the role of a guide , a friend and not just a disseminator of textbook knowledge. Our teachers are highly experienced with a minimum qualification of Bachelor’s Degree and D. El. Ed . Through our interactive and engaging classrooms we ensure that children are active participants rather than passive listeners.
Personal, academic, social and emotional development is given importance over basic cognitive development, ensuring that reasoning is not neglected. This interdisciplinary approach enables their all round development through the joy of learning , enduring values and the celebration of diversity .
